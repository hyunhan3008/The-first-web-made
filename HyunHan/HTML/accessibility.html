ACCESSIBILITY

STATMENT :

This webpage is fully committed to making it accessible to all, and removing barriers that prevent users from using our website.

The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) has produced guidelines for making web content accessible to people with disabilities. According to the guideline of the Royal National institute of the Blind (RNIB), we have used alt text where an image conveys essential information for the meaning of the page. Where an image is purley decorative we don't add alt text as this is superflous information, and extra 'noise' [for] a screenreader.

Should any user have difficulty in accessing the content of this site, please contact the Web Team and we will provide assistance or information in an alternative format. Details are informed in our contatc us page.
